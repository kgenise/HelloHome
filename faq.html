Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

I've found a home I like! Now what?

The first step in getting to say hello to your new home is contacting the agent who posted it. Only those logged in with a buyer account can view agent contact details. If you see a home you like, click on it to view its full details, and you'll see the agents contact information in the "Contact Us" section of a property.

How do I post a property?

Only agents can post properties. Once logged in with your agent account, you can click your profile icon in the top right corner and select "Post a Property". From there, just fill out all of the property details and click Post!

How do I edit my account information?

Once you're logged in, you can click your profile icon in the top right corner and click to edit your account. It is important to note that you cannot edit your email to be one that is already linked to an existing account.
